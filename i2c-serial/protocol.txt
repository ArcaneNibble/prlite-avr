all comms is in hex
upper case recommended, lower case should work but not tested

coming from module to pc, module has address FE
data is reported raw in hex

from pc to module:

+--------------------------------+---------------------------+-------------------------------------+---------------------------+
| 1 byte	num bytes to recieve | nnnn bytes	data to send | optional .	should recieve a byte? | space ' '	end of request |
+--------------------------------+---------------------------+-------------------------------------+---------------------------+

presence of '.' indicates that a response will be requested
reciving 0 bytes will crash (or probably recieve 256)

example:
'1002040004AAAA. '
| 10 | 02040004AAAA | . |   |
receive 16 bytes, send 02040004AAAA, do ask for reply, begin
